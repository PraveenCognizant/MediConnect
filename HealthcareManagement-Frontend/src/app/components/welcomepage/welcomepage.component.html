What We Offer

Everything you need in one place

MediConnect brings the entire healthcare journey online — from discovery to diagnosis.

Smart Appointment Booking

Browse available slots in real time, select your preferred doctor and time, and confirm your appointment in under 60 seconds. Receive instant status updates.

  • Real-time slot availability
  • Doctor-wise filtering
  • Instant booking confirmation

Digital Prescriptions

Your prescriptions are stored digitally and accessible from anywhere. Never lose a prescription again — view, download and share with pharmacies effortlessly.

  • Permanent prescription history
  • Doctor-signed digital records
  • Accessible from any device

Verified Doctor Profiles

Every doctor on MediConnect is verified by our admin team before they can accept patients. Browse by specialization, experience and availability.

  • Admin-verified credentials
  • Specialization search
  • Experience & hospital history

Secure Health Records

All patient data is protected with JWT-based authentication and BCrypt password encryption. Your health records are private and accessible only by you.

  • JWT token authentication
  • BCrypt password encryption
  • Role-based access control

Real-Time Slot Tracking

Check slot availability for any doctor before booking. Slots show Available, Pending (awaiting approval) or Booked — so you always know what's free.

  • Live slot status updates
  • No double-booking ever
  • Pending vs confirmed clarity

Admin Control Dashboard

A powerful admin panel to approve/reject doctors, monitor patients, view all appointments and manage the entire hospital ecosystem from one screen.

  • Doctor approval workflow
  • Patient & appointment overview
  • Full system analytics
